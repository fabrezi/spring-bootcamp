# Interview Questionaire

I must have taken a dozen java interview test. These are the occuring questions. Feel free to add more questions:

1. What is the difference between `String` and `StringBuffer` data type?

2. What is the difference between `Array` and `ArrayList`?

3. Count the occurrence of the word `the` in the lines:

```
The sheep jumped the fence,
The fox followed the sheep,
The hunter came after the fox,
The shadow followed the hunter,
```

4. How does the JVM works?

5. What is exception handling? Provide example.

6. What are the four fundamentals of OOP?
